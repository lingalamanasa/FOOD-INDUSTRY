Our Journey of Flavors

Stackly Foods opened its doors in the winter of 2018 with a simple goal: to serve food that delights the heart and inspires the imagination. What started as an intimate 10-table bistro has expanded into an internationally recognized sanctuary of fine dining, renowned for culinary artistry and impeccable hospitality.

Every plate we present is a canvas, combining seasonal flavors, precise styling, and premium ingredients. We are dedicated to building a space where families, friends, and food lovers can gather to create lifelong memories over incredible dining experiences.

Our cooking style is deeply rooted in classical French cuisine, fused with bold, modern, global culinary concepts to provide an unexpected, yet comforting dining adventure.

Our Core Values

We are governed by principles that guarantee excellence on every plate and in every guest interaction.

Our Mission

To serve culinary masterpieces crafted from organic, sustainable ingredients, elevating fine dining into an unforgettable sensory and aesthetic adventure.

Our Vision

To establish a global standard for luxury dining, recognized for our culinary creativity, sustainable food sourcing, and warm, unmatched guest experiences.

Absolute Quality

From the fields of local organic farmers to the fine detail of our presentation plates, we hold ourselves to the highest possible culinary standards.
